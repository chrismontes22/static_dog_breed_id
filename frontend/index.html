🦴 Common Questions

How do I identify my dog's breed?

Simply upload a clear photo of your dog. Our AI model analyses the image and returns the top 3 most likely breeds with confidence scores — no account or sign-up needed.

What dog breeds can Sniff & Tell identify?

Sniff & Tell recognises 73 dog breeds, including popular ones like Labrador Retriever, German Shepherd, Corgi, French Bulldog, Golden Retriever, and many more. Use the breed browser above to see the full list.

Can I ask questions about a dog breed after identification?

Yes! After identifying the breed, ask anything — exercise requirements, temperament, grooming needs, diet tips, health concerns, and more. Our AI knowledge base provides detailed, helpful answers sourced from expert breed data and live web search.

Is Sniff & Tell free to use?

Completely free — no account, sign-up, or credit card required. Upload a photo and get instant AI-powered breed identification at no cost.

How accurate is the dog breed identifier?

Sniff & Tell uses a deep learning model trained on 73 breeds and returns the top 3 most likely matches with confidence scores. For best results use a clear, well-lit photo of your dog facing the camera.

Does Sniff & Tell store or save my photos?

No. Your photo is transmitted directly to the breed classifier and discarded immediately after the prediction result is returned. We never store, retain, or use your images for any other purpose.
